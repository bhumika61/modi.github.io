hostel

MIMT offers comprehensive and independent hostels for Girls and Boys along with all modern amenities. A separate magnificent hostel for Girls in the college campus with hostel warden in each hostel to supervise day to day management

  1. Modi Girls Hostel, Modi College, Dadabari, Kota
  2. Modi Boys Hostel, C-259, Talwandi, Kota
  3. Modi Residency, 507, New Rajeev Gandhi Nagar, Kota

Hostel accommodation is however considered on the basis of both need and availability. Hostel rules have been framed keeping in mind the local culture and traditions, with a view to guide and help students to attain standards of implacable conduct.
