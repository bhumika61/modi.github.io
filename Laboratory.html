Laboratory

MIMT has the best computer laboratory, with the latest computing facilities and high speed internet connectivity. The Institute has four computer labs with over 400 computers and 25 MBPS 1:1 network. Also the laboratories are running intranet of the institute and the institute systems works with an ERP technology backbone. The entire campus network is connected with latest technology enabled servers and CISCO network equipments. The campus is Wi-Fi enabled.
