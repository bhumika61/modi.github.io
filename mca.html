Master of Computer Applications

The Department of Computer Applications is one of the pioneering Departments of the Institute that offers Information Technology related courses such as MCA and is one among the top institutions offering MCA courses in the state of Rajasthan. It is committed to the cause of quality education in Information Technology, a field growing in leaps and bounds. It provides a good theoretical foundation through high quality teaching complemented by extensive practical training. It is dedicated to the mission of providing honest sincere, principle oriented and highly skilled professionals trained to adapt to the dynamic requirement of the IT industry. This Department currently offers MCA (Three years full-time) and MCA- Lateral Entry (Two years full-time) Postgraduate Programmes.

Objectives

  1. To train the students to analyze problems in a wide range of applications
  2. To expose students to enterprise software management methodologies

Scope for Employment

  1. Application Software Development, Testing and Maintenance
  2. Database Administrators
  3. Software Developers and Entrepreneurs
  4. Programmers and Software consultants
