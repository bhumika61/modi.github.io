Welcome to Modi Institute of Management & Technology

The world around us is witnessing rapid change in various fields and education is no exception. Technical and management education in INDIA needs to anticipate the future and carve out appropriate strategies and programs, to enable students to be able to meet the challenges of tomorrow. Modi Institute of Management & Technology (MIMT), Kota is committed to this endeavor.
