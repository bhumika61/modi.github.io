Master of Business Administration

The Modi Institute of Management and Technology, Kota (MIMT) has been operating two-year full time MBA program recognized by AICTE and affiliated to Rajasthan Technical University, Kota. The institute offers MBA degree with Dual Specialization in all leading branches of functional management. Presently the student intake capacity is 120 Nos. The faculty intellectuals believe whether it is technological education or multi directional management education, it does not equip the student to face real situation problems confidently and successfully unless backed by a massive knowledge of problem solving techniques and supported by an approach to visualize and analyze problems on individual basis. Thus inline with this conception, greater emphasis is laid on case studies and formation/presentation of case in the classroom studies besides routine theory lectures. Eventually, the institute predominantly believes in building "brand equity" in management education on "learning through case studies".

Objectives

  1. To develope Management Skills and Leadership Skills in students
  2. Ability of demonstrate to assess and evaluate the dynamic internal and external elements of the competitive global environment
  3. To develop Problem Solving and Decision Making Skills in students

Scope for Employment

  1. MBA graduate is unparalleled. Apart from attractive pay packets and placements being offered at college-level itself, it opens doors to various sectorial opportunities
  2. One can opt for entrepreneurship and become self-dependent, work in co-operatives and business houses as the opportunities are literally unlimited
  3. Management Graduate gets absorbed into a Company through the Campus Placement Program of the Modi Institute of Management & Technology
