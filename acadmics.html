About MIMT

MIMT is known for its academic excellence, accomplished faculty and leading edge technology development for the future. MIMT prepares its students for career success. Students are educated in fundamentals as well as emerging technologies and developments; they are trained to be adaptable and flexible for the rapidly changing world. MIMT offers students a dynamic, supportive and creative environment in which they are encouraged by a committed staff, to pursue personal excellence in academic pursuits, resulting in enhanced prospects for career development. MIMT is committed to provide all students with quality program that will meet and exceed expectations.

The Institute, since its inception has aimed at producing the best talent from its premises by imparting sound academic and professional knowledge to the students, using innovative methodology of teaching and learning.The Institute has a city centered campus in an area of 10 acre. The campus environment is highly conducive and healthy for the all-round development of the student. The Institute is preparing professionals to learn the process of combating every known or unknown challenge of their career. This is done through highly experienced and dedicated faculty members, eminent industry experts for guest lectures, exposure to practical knowledge through workshops, seminars and industrial training and consultancy projects.

This Institute gives full assistance and support to get the students placed in the reputed companies. Ethical and moral values are inculcated to make them integral citizen for exhibiting values against pressure, injustice and greed. MIMT offers Master's Degree Programs like MBA and MCA along with Ph.D. & EVA (Extra Value Added) Programs.
